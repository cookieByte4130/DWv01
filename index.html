Let me tell you a story Hero Name...
A long time ago, dragons were powerful. When we had the relic of Tanyuan, we gave our fellow creatures luck.
Then Ganon came.
Originally he called everyone together, celebrating our own elemental roots.
The celebration quickly dissolved into hate and fear of others. Each element began to think themselves better than the others.
Fearing that we dragons would bless their enemies more than themselves, they broke apart the Tanyuan relic into four runes.
The rune of each element was given to their respective land. Each land entrusted a champion to keep their rune safe from others.
Ganon is growing stronger feeding off of the hate that divided the land. He has already begun his quest to destroy all of our kind.
One day a hero will unite all the runes and defeat Ganon...
